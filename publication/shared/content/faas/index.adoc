= Flight Assessment and Alert System
:notitle:

This chapter covers the Flight Assessment and Alerting Service (FAAS) — the Network capability for identifying flights
that meet predefined safety, security or operational monitoring criteria, and for applying the appropriate operational
responses.

FAAS unifies activities currently implemented through several independent mechanisms within the Network Manager and
across operational stakeholders. The unified capability supports three kinds of operational response — stakeholder
alerting, operational warnings and highlighting, and operational control actions on flight plan messages — alongside
the supporting traceability and reporting that any monitoring service requires.

_Within each section, Operational Needs are presented first, followed by the Operational Requirements that implement
them._
