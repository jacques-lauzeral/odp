= Crisis
:notitle:

This chapter covers the Crisis domain — the Network capabilities supporting operational stakeholders during crisis
situations and during regular crisis exercises. It is organised around two areas of operational concern: the
xref:crisis/crisis_information_portal/index.adoc[Crisis Information Portal], through which crisis-related operational
information is shared and managed; and xref:crisis/conflict_zones/index.adoc[Conflict Zones], through which
authoritative information on airspace closures, restrictions and safety advisories is consolidated and made available
across the Network.

Operational management of the Crisis Information Portal — the primary channel through which NM, ANSPs, Airspace Users
and other operational stakeholders share crisis-related operational information, including dedicated support for crisis
exercises.

Consolidation and structured visualisation of conflict-zone information from authoritative sources (NOTAMs, EASA SIBs, CZIBs, SFARs, AICs and AIPs), supporting situational awareness and operational decision-making across the Network.

_Within each section, Operational Needs are presented first, followed by the Operational Requirements that implement
them._
