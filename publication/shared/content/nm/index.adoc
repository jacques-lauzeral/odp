= Transversal NM
:notitle:

NM operates as a critical service across the European aviation Network. Its services need to be delivered with
consistency, reliability and trust, regardless of which channel a stakeholder uses to access them. This chapter defines
the Operational Needs and Operational Requirements that apply transversally across all NM service delivery channels -
NMUI, NM B2B, and any future channel through which NM services are delivered.

Without a transversal layer, every channel-specific or domain-specific chapter would have to reinvent fundamental
concerns: how services evolve, how they remain available, how they are secured, who uses them and how, what they say.
This chapter expresses those concerns once, authoritatively, so that channel-specific and domain-specific chapters can
focus on what is genuinely specific to them. The principles defined here govern by default; channel-specific chapters
refine them for their channel where useful, and take precedence within their channel provided they do not contradict
the transversal principle.

The chapter is organised around five themes: Service definition and evolution, Operational behaviour, Compliance and
traceability, Users and collaboration, and Presentation and semantics.

_Within each section, Operational Needs are presented first, followed by the Operational Requirements that implement
them._
